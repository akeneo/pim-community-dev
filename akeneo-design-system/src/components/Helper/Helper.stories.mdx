# Helper

## Usage

Allows to help the user by pushing contextual information.

### Big helper

Located at the top of a page, it helps the user on the page's features. A link guides the user to additional information. *i.e., the help center*

### Small helper

Located at the top of a section, just below the section title, it informs the user about the features of the section.

### Inline helper

Located just below a field, it indicates to the user:
- The information expected in the field;
- An alert on the information filled in by the user;
- An error related to the information filled in by the user.
